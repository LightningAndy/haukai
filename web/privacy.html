The Haukai Restaurant Privacy Statement

In accordance with the NZ Privacy Act, we collect personal information from you including information about your:
Full Name
Contact Information
Billing Information
Purchase History

We collect your personal information in order to:
Send you information regarding your booking.
Contact you in the event:
Your booking is cancelled or you fail to make your reservation time.
Send you information regarding your purchases.

Besides our staff, we share this information with:
PayPal in order to process financial transactions.

Providing information is optional. If you choose not to enter the required information, we will be unable to process your booking or purchase. We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it using a thrird party contractor.

Under the Official Information Act 1982 you have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us here.
