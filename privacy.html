We collect personal information from you, including information about your:
  • Name
  • Contact Information
  • Billing Information
  • Purchase History

We collect your personal information in order to:
  • Send you information regarding your booking.
  • Contact you in the event:
    1. Your booking is cancelled.
    2. You fail to make your reserved time.
  • Send you information regarding your purchases.

Besides our staff, we share this information with:
  • PayPal in order to process finical transactions.

Providing information is optional. If you choose not to enter the required information, we will be unable to process your booking or purchase. We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by 3rd party contractor.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us here.
